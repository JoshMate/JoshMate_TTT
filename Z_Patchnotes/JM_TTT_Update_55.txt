################################
#### TTT Update 55 - Patch Notes
################################

[Overhaul: Ammo]
- There are now only 2 Ammo Types (used to be 3)
- Light Ammo (Max 60) SMG, Light Pistol, Rifle, etc...
- Heavy Ammo (Max 10) Heavy Pistol, Shotgun, Sniper, etc...
- You start with Max Ammo for both types
- Ammo no longer spawns on maps

[Overhaul: Grenades]
- Their models have been reworked to be clearer which grenade you are holding
- They now all Deploy instantly, so they can be used much faster than before
- All Grenades now leave behind a mark on the floor where they exploded
- All Grenades now have improved net-code and visual effects
- New Grenade types, See later on in the patch notes for details

[Overhaul: Weapon Placement]
- I have PAINSTAKINGLY gone through and re-placed weapons on ALL the maps
(This took me about 6 hours to do... FML)
- This is to incorporate the new weapons / ammo changes
- I have balanced all maps around the LMG and Sniper being rare and pistols common
- I have also placed about 10x more Care package / Objective Spawns for variety

[Overhaul: Game Modes]
- I have written a Gamemode management system that will be in charge of
making sure Gamemodes happen randomly but are not too common or too rare.
- Game modes have a base 1/8 chance that increases each round without one
- Fixed Objectives spawning in Unreachable areas and improved their overall placements

[Glue Grenade]
- A new basic grenade that appears around the map
- A single use impact grenade that slows players in it's radius

[Healing Grenade]
- A new basic grenade that appears around the map
- A single use temporary buff that heals you over time
(The same as Sage's self heal from Valorant)

[GameMode: Defuse the Bomb]
- The amount of bombs that appear now depends on how many people are alive
- To defuse a bomb you must press E on it with only bare hands equipped

[GameMode: Protect the Files]
- Attempting to destroy files as an innocent is impossible and no longer kills you
- To defuse a bomb you must press E on it with only bare hands equipped

[All Pistols]
- Buffed movementspeed buff while equipped: +0% -> +10%

[LMG]
- Buffed Damage: 20 -> 30
- Buffed movespeed penalty while equipped: -20% -> -10%
- Buffed Bullets Pushing Force: 20 -> 50
- This is now considered a power weapon and spawns rarely on maps

[Tag Grenade]
- Buffed Tracking duration: 4 -> 5 Seconds

[Jump Grenade]
- No longer have a half a second delay before pushing you upwards

[Barrel Swep]
- Barrels no longer block player movement

[Carepackage Loot: Rooty-Tooty]
- Has now been soo heavily buffed it's an ABSOLUTE BEAST
- Is now a single shot one use weapon that can't be reloaded

[Karma]
- Being at max Karma now grants you a +5 HP bonus ontop of the previous +25 for +30
- Improved the reward messages for Karma
