Josh Mate TTT Update 65 - Patch Notes
###################################
#### New Stuff
###################################
 
[Awp]
- AWP has received a total rework 
- The AWP is now very loud and fires a tracer round
- Direct hits are insta-kill and perfectly accurate
- Bullets also explode on impact dealing an additional 60 DMG in a small radius
- New icon

[Throwing Knives]
- Knife has received a total rework and is now Throwing Knives
- You get 3 of them and they can only be thrown
- The further they fly, the more damage they do (No damage cap)
- They leave a faint trail behind them but are completely silent
- Built in system that judges your trick shots and tells you how you did
- New Icon

[Glue Gun]
- Glue Gun has been reworked
- A Detective shop item
- It's bullet explode on impact infflicting the Glue effect
- Does fairly low DPS compared to other guns, but has splash damage
- Goes in the primary weapon slot and uses Light Ammo
